Choosing your own adventure

The aim of this project is to design and plan the creation and implementation of a website that exists to host a “Choose your Own Adventure” (CYOA) type game that provides an engaging and satisfying user experience to those who visit it. A CYOA game is a game wherein a story is told from a second-person point of view, where the reader assumes the role of the main character of the story. The reader is presented with multiple choices throughout the story where they make a decision that impacts where the story takes them as well as the outcome of the tale, resulting in a unique experience (as well as being highly repayable).

Classically, these types of games were presented through books, where the reader would be presented with a choice, flipping to whatever page instructed by the choice they make. Presenting this type of game using a website will modernise the user experience by attempting to immerse the user further through the use of the endless features of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

The Story of the Game

In the far future, a secret agency has successfully developed time travel technology. This technology is far from perfect but is capable of transferring the user up to a few hours back in time. Despite this not being a significant amount of time, this agency has been using it for significant purposes, doing what they can to see that mankind stays on the right path, secretly deploying their agents all over the world to see that events go to plan, and if they don’t, to rewind and try again until they do.

In the story presented on this website, the user will play the role of one of these “Time Agents”. Their mission is to infiltrate a foreign research facility that is on the cusp of developing time travel technology of their own. The Agency can see no good coming from this and has decided that an agent must be deployed to ensure this progress is sabotaged completely.

The user will have to successfully infiltrated the research facility and find a way to sabotage the progress made on this technology - using only their wits and little bit of luck!
